# Current Capstone Project

## Improving Veteran Healthcare Through Mortality Analytics

**Dataset**

2018–2023 VA Veteran All-Cause Mortality Dataset

**Business Problem**

The Veterans Health Administration continues to face challenges in reducing preventable mortality among veterans. My capstone examines mortality patterns, age-adjusted mortality rates, and Years of Potential Life Lost (YPLL) to identify trends that may support evidence-based healthcare planning and suicide prevention.
